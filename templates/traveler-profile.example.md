## Travel principles

These shape every plan. Override per-trip when needed.

1. **Long-form over weekenders.** Default duration: 14–24 days for big trips, 5–10 for short.
2. **Cadence 4:1.** 4 active days + 1 rest day. Rest = wellness, charming village, light excursion — not "do nothing".
3. **Start early.** Treks at 7–8h to avoid crowds and afternoon thunderstorms.
4. **One highlight per day.** Don't stack 6 attractions; one main thing, rest orbits around it.
5. **+30% drive margins.** Mountain/scenic roads take longer than Google says.
6. **Cook + restaurant mix.** Local-market shopping for most meals; restaurants 4–5x per trip, not daily.
7. **Book what sells out.** Cable cars, toll roads, popular restaurants — months ahead. Everything else, decide on the day.
8. **Mid-to-upper budget.** Not luxury. 5–10% emergency buffer always.
9. **No beach, no resort, no generic itineraries.** _(adjust to your tastes — this is a sample bias)_
